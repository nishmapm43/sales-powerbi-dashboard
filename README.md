# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard — Power BI

## Business Question
**Which product category and region should we prioritise next quarter — and why?**

## Dashboard Elements
- **4 KPI Cards** — Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Delivery Rate
- **Bar Chart** — Sales by Category (Electronics vs Furniture vs Stationery)
- **Line Chart** — Monthly Sales Trend (January–December)
- **Donut Chart** — Order Status Breakdown (Delivered/Pending/Cancelled)
- **Column Chart** — Profit by Region (North/South/East/West)
- **2 Interactive Slicers** — Region + Category (filters all visuals simultaneously)

## Key Insights
- Electronics = 71% of total revenue — highest priority category
- June is the peak sales month — plan campaigns before June
- 76% orders delivered — 16.3% revenue (₹2,22,900) at risk from undelivered orders
- North region leads profit — East is lowest and needs attention

## Recommendation
Double down on Electronics in North and South regions for Q2.
Investigate delivery failures in East region to recover at-risk revenue.

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop · DAX Measures · Interactive Slicers · Data Visualisation
